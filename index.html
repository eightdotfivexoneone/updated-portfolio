About

My work centers around collaboration. It is the best way I have found to stay inspired and stay human. Several of my most recent projects have been taking shape as participatory events in which I provide sets of instructions and/or tools with which participants react to a specific moment or place. Participants responses are then recorded and processed into a document commemorating the moment.

Recently I have begun teaching myself generative art and design. I like to think of code as a similar set of instructions. Through code an individual can explore thousands of possibilities in a moment. In March I began an intensive six month full-stack programming boot camp at UNH to both expand my marketable skills as well as build a proper foundation in how to learn code. I believe that generative approaches are a central component of the future of design and need to be integrated into undergraduate curriculum. Once I have command of where this research leads me I am excited to bring these new tools into my classroom and workshops.
